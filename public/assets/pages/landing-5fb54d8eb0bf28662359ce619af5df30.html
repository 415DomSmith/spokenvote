How it Works

1. Find a group

Start by finding your group in the top box. Once you locate your group, click a topic to see its proposals.


2. See what they're proposing

If the topic has not been started yet, click "Start New Topic".


3. Help the best ideas reach consensus

In a topic you can add your support to any existing proposal or create an improved version using any proposal as your starting point.


As each participant contributes either support to an existing version or creates a new improved version, the group collectively moves toward reaching a majority opinion together. As the wording improves, the best proposals gain momentum by receiving support from multiple users and forming a clarity of consensus.

Why it Matters

Spokenvote is a tool to help groups of any size, from a local school board to an entire nation’s people, reach consensus with radical efficiency. We think the process should be intuitive, fun, and yes, democratic.

Deeply inspired by Wikipedia, Spokenvote is an open source non-profit intended for small groups today, but over time to reach national politics. It starts with spontaneously capturing ideas as they occur to us. When we do that as a diverse group, a “power of the crowd” effect is introduced. The result is often a revelation of common sense that is difficult to discover from a top-down approach.
